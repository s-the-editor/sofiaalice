About




Sofia Alice is a writer, editor, artist, and software engineer. She grew up in Melrose, Massachusetts. In 2024, she graduated from Emory University with a BS in Computer Science and a BA in English and Creative Writing. She received High Honors for the completion of her honors thesis, "Ghost Hunt," a novella. She now lives in New Hampshire and works as a Software Engineer. Her dream is to be a full-time author of fantasy novels.


On top of software engineering, Sofia works as a freelance fiction editor on Fiverr. She has a 4.9-star rating and 50+ completed orders.


In her free time, she loves making art. Her current obsessions include costume making, wire-wrapped jewelry, stamp carving, letterboxing, zine making, painting, and web development (ha).


Sofia is also working on the high fantasy novel Saga of the Necromancer.
